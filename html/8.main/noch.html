[dataout]=noch(datain,F1,F2,F3,F4)

noch filter

Frequencies <=F1 and >=F4 are untouched
Frequencies between F2 and F3 are zeroed
Frequencies between F2 and F1 as well as between F3 and F4 are linearily
reduced

 All frequencies are in Hertz (Hz)
